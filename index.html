Our Philosophy

We Cook With
Intention

At Chefaleh, we don't just focus on flavor — we focus on what goes into every ingredient. We cook exclusively with extra virgin olive oil and avocado oil — never seed oils.

We use unbleached, organic flours and avoid processed shortcuts. No soy-based fillers, no unnecessary additives, no compromises.

Real ingredients, properly sourced, cooked with intention. This isn't about trends — it's about quality, digestion, and how food should actually be prepared.

Our Story

Elevated Traditions,
Curated for Your Table

Chefaleh is a private chef–driven culinary studio focused on refined, high-quality Shabbat and event dining. Our roots are in hospitality at the highest level — from curated travel experiences to private villa programs — where food is not just served, but orchestrated.

Built by a team with experience producing high-end culinary programs across the U.S. and Europe, our style is rooted in bold, clean flavors with an Israeli and Mediterranean influence.
